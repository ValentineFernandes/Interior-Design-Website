We Are The Finest Interior Designing Firm In Your City

iDESIGN is the world's largest soft furnishing fabrics. We are proud to be globally local,understanding the aesthetic sensibilities of every country we touch. Since our inception in 1999,we have grown to become the preferred partner for the world's most prestigious furniture and furnishings manufacturers. We are defined by a commitment to excellence and a passion to bring dreams to reality that meld expertise with art. In India, Brand iDESIGN enjoys a leadership postion in its category, with a strong retail presence throughout the country with a wide network of multi-brand outlets, an ever increasing number of exclusive iDESIGN stores and our e-commerce platform.
