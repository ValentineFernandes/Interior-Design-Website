Living Areas Will Accompany Open Kitchens In Design Trends For 2022

As we have started spending more time at home, we want its design to be more suitable. One such personalisation aspect is the layout of your kitchen. Since all of us have turned into home chefs (thanks to YouTube and Reels), we want our kitchen to be a part of our living areas and not sitetracked. As a result, open kitchen extensions in living rooms are currently a worldwide craze! Such a kitchen design lets you cook, stay in the living area and not get dissociated from the rest of the house. So yes, bring in open kitchen layouts!

Double-Decker Beds For Kids

The design of your kid’s room plays a crucial role in their growth and development. They’re at an age when their receptivity is maximum. They pick up on things from their surroundings that stay with them for life. So, having them spend most of their time in a positive and encouraging environment can be key to forming a strong character and personality. Double-decker beds for kids are a great way to give their room a clutter-free look. It also frees up space for a study desk or indoor play area. The unique design is versatile and comes with intelligent storage space. The double-decker bed design features wall-mounted shelves and several cupboards to easily arrange books, stationery and clothes.
